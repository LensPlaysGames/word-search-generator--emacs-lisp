* SearchGen

A word search puzzle generator written in Emacs LISP.

Capable of producing TeX such that a PDF may be made, if you are into the physical printing sort of thing. Also can produce plain-text representation.

Capable of altering directions and fill characters.

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
  (searchgen-advanced '("cat" "dog" "bat" "rat" "bear" "lion") :size 4)
#+end_src
#+begin_example
r n r a t
t a o r p
g a e i e
o r c b l
d o t a b
#+end_example

#+begin_src emacs-lisp
  (searchgen-basic '("cat" "dog" "bat" "rat" "bear" "lion"))
#+end_src
#+begin_example
o o o f b
b a t d e
c a t o a
r a t g r
h l i o n
#+end_example
